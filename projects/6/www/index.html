What are Opera Warriors ?

Opera Warriors are a collection of programmatically, randomly generated NFTs based on the Ethereum blockchain. The 1st generation consists of 10,000 randomly assembled warriors from over 100M total options. All the elements of Opera Warriors come from the trait of traditional Peking Operas. Each Opera Warrior is comprised of a unique color, eyes, eyebrow, cheek, chin-the possibilities are endless!

How tough is my warrior ?

First of all, all warriors are brave enough and qualified to fight in the ancient arena. Each warrior has 4 basic combat attributes——minATK, maxATK, DEF, HP. The points of each attribute are determined by which elements your warrior is made up of.
These attributes are vital when they fight in the arena, and they’ll be even more important for breeding new warriors.
Beware, 108 famous heroes active throughout ancient Chinese history will be hidden among all 10,000 warriors. These heroes will be more powerful in the battle. If you’re lucky enough to get one, make sure you hold on to it, and make the most advantage of it.

What’s an NFT ?
An NFT stands for "Non-fungible token" and is a fancy way of saying it's a unique, digital item type that users can buy, own, and trade. NFTs are fantastic digital art and additional utility like exclusive access to websites or participation in an event. Think of it like a rare piece of art that can also act as a "members" card.
What is Metamask ?
Metamask is a crypto wallet that can store your Ethereum and is needed to purchase and mint an Opera Warrior. Having a wallet gives you an Ethereum address (i.e., 0x71F5….8529); this is where your NFT will be stored. Learn more about Metamask and how easy it is to use over here! (https://metamask.io/)
